# Custom Races

This document contains homebrew races for our campaign.

## Shadowborn

*Medium humanoid*

The Shadowborn are descendants of mortals who made pacts with entities from the Shadowfell. They carry the mark of darkness in their very essence.

### Traits

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Dexterity score increases by 2, and your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Age:** Shadowborn mature at the same rate as humans but live slightly longer, up to 120 years.

**Size:** Medium

**Speed:** 30 feet

**Darkvision:** You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.

### Special Abilities

| Level | Ability | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Shadow Step | Once per long rest, teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see that is in dim light or darkness |
| 5 | Cloak of Shadows | You can cast *invisibility* on yourself once per long rest |
| 10 | Shadow Merge | You can move through solid objects as if they were difficult terrain (once per long rest) |

> **Note:** These abilities require you to be in dim light or darkness to activate.

## Crystalkin

*Medium humanoid (construct)*

Born from the heart of ancient magical crystals, Crystalkin are living gemstones given humanoid form.

### Traits

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Constitution score increases by 2, and your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Age:** Crystalkin do not age in the traditional sense. They can live for millennia if not destroyed.

**Size:** Medium

**Speed:** 25 feet

**Natural Armor:** Your crystalline body provides natural protection. When not wearing armor, your AC equals 13 + your Dexterity modifier.

### Crystal Resonance

You can attune to one of the following crystal types at character creation:

- **Ruby (Fire):** Resistance to fire damage
- **Sapphire (Cold):** Resistance to cold damage
- **Emerald (Poison):** Resistance to poison damage
- **Amethyst (Psychic):** Resistance to psychic damage

### Weaknesses

```
Critical Vulnerability: Sonic damage deals an additional 1d6 damage to you.
Sunlight Sensitivity: You have disadvantage on attack rolls and
Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight when you, the target
of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.
```
